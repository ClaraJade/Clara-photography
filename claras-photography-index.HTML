About Clara

Hi! I'm Clara Jenkins, a 16-year-old photographer based in Nashville, Tennessee. I'm passionate about capturing the moments that define your high school experience—the victories, celebrations, and memories you'll treasure for a lifetime.

With a keen eye for composition and an understanding of what makes these moments special, I bring both technical skill and genuine passion to every session. Whether it's a game-winning shot, a prom night glow, or a perfect senior portrait, I'm here to create images that tell your story.

Let's work together to capture the magic of your high school years.
